Vasiliki

Harsh Noise Duo

About

Vasiliki are the harsh noise duo responsible for the creation of Over the Moon. Back in 2022, they founded the label in order to distribute they debut tape, and eventually to bring other artists under their wing in order make sure they weren't exploited.

Little is known about their real identities, even to other OTM artists and their collaborators. They are simply known as "E" and "J". Their music has heavy focus on the ocean and societal critique.
