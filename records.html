The Label

Over the Moon Records is the music branch of our collective, featuring eclectic and experimental musicians with brilliant producers and engineers to bring their visions to life. We allow artists full control over every part of the music making and marketing process. Every single creator has equal say about how OTM continues into the future.

Over the Moon Records was established in 2022 by a duo of experimental noise musicians and has since grown into a family of creatives supporting each other's work.
