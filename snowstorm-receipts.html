Snowstorm Receipts

EDM Trio

About

Snowstorm Receipts is a trio of electronic producers. They formed in 2023 in Middlebury, Vermont. Their music is defined by lots of fun hooks, bright synths and general experimentation with the conventions of pop music.

The three members of Snowstorm Receipts are lifelong friends and have stated that they always shared a certain connected creative energy. As such, this is only one of their group endeavours, as they have created for all corners of the art world in one way or another. Music, however, always keeps them coming back.
