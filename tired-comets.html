Tired Comets

Post-Rock Band

About

Tired Comets is a post-rock (and sometimes just regular rock) band featuring major influences from places like midwest emo and dream pop. The band formed in 2021 under the creative leadership of the late, great Sofia Serdyukova. Following a hasty promotional EP, the band committed themselves to playing shows whenever they could to gain exposure. In late 2022, they released their acclaimed debut album "Invited to Something Bad".
