Sofia Serdyukova

R.I.P.

About

Sofia Serdyukova (София "Софушка" Сердюкова) was born in Vorkuta, Komi Republic. She showed musical interest from a very young age, learning to play her father's keyboard. Her family moved to the U.S. in 2015, and from there she would grow her talent, eventually creating the band Tired Comets with some close friends. She was instrumental to the Breakfast for Dinosaurs collective's initial formation, and spent much of her time supporting her friends, despite her own struggles with mental health.

She was the cornerstone for BfD's philosophy of equality. We miss her every day. She inspired everyone in this collective to follow their dreams. Fly high, angel.
